AMAZON GIFT CARD TERMS OF USE

What is a Gift Card?
Amazon.com.tr gift cards are digital or physical codes that you can load into your account as a balance and use instead of cash for your purchases on Amazon.

How to Install and Use?
1. Go to My Account > Gift Cards tab.
2. Click the "Add Gift Card" button and enter the 14-15 digit code on your card.
3. The balance is reflected in your account instantly.
4. During the payment phase, the system will automatically offer to deduct the purchase amount from your gift card balance. 

Rules and Limitations:
- Validity Period: The expiration date of Amazon.com.tr gift cards is 10 years from the date of issue.
- Non-transferable: You cannot transfer the balance of a gift card you have added to your account to another account, convert it into cash, or receive a refund to another credit card.
- Unusable Products: Another gift card or some digital memberships cannot be purchased with gift card balance.
- Insufficient Balance: If your shopping amount is higher than your gift card balance, you can complete the remaining amount with your credit card or debit card.

Refund:
When you return a product you purchased with a gift card balance, the price of the product is instantly refunded to your account as a gift card balance. It is not refundable in cash.
