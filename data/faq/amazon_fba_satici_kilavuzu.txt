AMAZON LOGISTICS (FBA) AND SELLER GUIDE

What is Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)?
Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) is a system where marketplace sellers send their products to Amazon warehouses and all processes such as storage, packaging, shipping and customer services are managed by Amazon.

FBA Advantages:
1. Sellers' products instantly gain the "Prime" label and benefit from free/fast shipping.
2. Customer returns and shipping issues are completely resolved by Amazon Customer Service.
3. Buy Box greatly increases the odds of winning.

Obligations of Sellers:
- It is the seller's responsibility to barcode and package the products to be sent to Amazon warehouses.
- For non-FBA (FBM - seller's own) products, customer messages must be responded to within 24 hours at the latest. Otherwise, the seller score will decrease.

Commissions and Deductions:
Amazon charges referral fees (commissions) at different rates depending on each product category sold. Sellers using FBA are also charged storage and shipping fees depending on the volume and weight of the product. An extra "long-term storage fee" is charged for products that remain unsold in the warehouse for a long time (more than 365 days).
