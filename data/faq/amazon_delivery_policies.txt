AMAZON.COM.TR DELIVERY AND SHIPPING POLICY

Shipping Times and Options:
Amazon.com.tr offers different delivery options to its customers:
1. Standard Delivery: It takes 2 to 4 business days nationwide for most items.
2. Same Day Delivery: Available for Amazon Prime members. It is only valid for products labeled "Same Day Delivery" delivered in certain major cities (Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir, etc.) and before certain hours (usually 12:00 noon).
3. Next Day Delivery: Available for Amazon Prime members. It offers customers in certain regions the opportunity to have their orders delivered the next business day.

Shipping Fees:
- Amazon Prime Members: They benefit from FREE and Fast Shipping, regardless of the basket amount, on products sold and sent by Amazon.com.tr or with the Amazon Logistics logo.
- Standard Customers: Standard shipping fee is charged for orders below a certain basket amount (e.g. 300 TL). Shipping is free for baskets over this amount. (Third party seller shipments may not be included in this scope).

Cargo Tracking:
After your order is shipped, you can see the live status of your cargo and the delivery company by clicking the "Track Cargo" button from the "My Orders" menu.
Among the cargo companies that Amazon Turkey works with are Kolay Gelsin, MNG Kargo, Aras Kargo, DHL and AGT (Same Day Delivery) courier services.

Undeliverable Packages:
The shipping company returns the package to Amazon in the following cases:
- If the address is missing or incorrect,
- If the cargo company cannot reach the recipient (usually delivery is attempted 2 times or a notice is left for pickup at the cargo branch),
- If the package is found to be damaged.
Once undeliverable packages return to our warehouse, your full refund will be automatically processed. These orders cannot be resent, if you want to buy the product again, you must create a new order.

International Deliveries (Amazon Global):
Delivery time for orders coming from abroad may take between 7 and 14 days depending on the country and customs density.
For Amazon Global orders, all customs duties and import charges (Import Fee Deposit) are calculated and collected upfront by Amazon at the cart stage. You will not be asked for any extra taxes, customs fees or shipping fees at the door.

Missing or Damaged on Delivery:
If you notice any crushing, wetting or tearing on the outer box of your package while receiving it from the cargo officer, you can file a "Damage Report".
If you find that the product is missing or damaged when you open the box, you can request an immediate refund or exchange by contacting Customer Services within 30 days.
