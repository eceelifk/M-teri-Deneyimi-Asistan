Why wasn't my package delivered?
A package may be undeliverable and returned due to:
An incorrect, incomplete or outdated address.
Unsuccessful delivery attempts by the cargo company.
The buyer may have refused delivery.
The address label became illegible during transportation.
The package was damaged during shipping.
The shipment is found to contain products with quality issues.
Delivery restrictions (for example, a correctional facility).
The driver has safety concerns because there is a dog at the address.
