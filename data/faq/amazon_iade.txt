Amazon Turkey Return Policy and User Rights

General Return Policy:
- 30-Day Return Period: Most products purchased through Amazon.com.tr can be returned unconditionally within 30 days from the delivery date.
- Product Condition: The products to be returned are expected to be in their original condition, unused and complete. New products must be returned new, with intact packaging and all accessories.
- Return Process: You can initiate your return process by going to the "My Orders" section in your account and using the "Return products" or "Return" option.

Special Cases and Exceptions:
- Defective/Damaged Products: The return process for incorrect, damaged or defective products may vary and is generally more flexible. In such cases, support may be provided beyond the standard 30-day period.
- Third Party Sellers: The return policies of other sellers selling through Amazon must also comply with Amazon's general policy. However, there may be situations where you need to contact the seller directly during the return process.
- Exceptional Categories: The return of some product categories may be subject to special conditions or excluded from the scope of return due to hygiene or the nature of the product.

Initiating a Return:
To see the most up-to-date and specific return options:
1. Log in to your Amazon.com.tr account.
2. Go to the "My Orders" page.
3. Select the product you want to return and follow the instructions by clicking the "Return products" button.
