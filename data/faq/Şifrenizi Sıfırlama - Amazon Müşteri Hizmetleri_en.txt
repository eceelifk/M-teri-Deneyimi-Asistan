Resetting Your Password
If you have forgotten your password, you can reset your password by following the password help process on our website.
To reset your password, go to .
1. When prompted, enter your email address or mobile phone number associated with your Amazon account and Continue
Select et.
To verify your request, we will send you an email or SMS with a One Time Password (OTP) (your chosen verification code).
We will send according to the method.
2. Enter the One Time Password you received and select Continue.
3. Create a new password.
The new password will be valid from the moment it is created. The new password will change all passwords associated with the same email address.
It will be valid for Amazon accounts.
