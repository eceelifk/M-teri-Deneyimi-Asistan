AMAZON CAMPAIGN, PRIME DAY AND SMILE FRIDAY RULES

Major Campaign Periods:
Amazon.com.tr organizes two huge campaign periods every year: "Prime Day" (only for Prime members) and "Smile Friday" (Black Friday).

Campaign Period Differences:
1. Shipping Times: Due to density during these periods, there may be 1-3 day delays in standard and Prime shipping times. The delivery date is clearly stated on the order screen.
2. Price Drop Refund: Amazon.com.tr does not have a "Price Guarantee" policy. If the price of the product drops after you receive it, Amazon will not refund the difference. If the customer wishes, he can return the product and order it again at the new price.
3. Stock Availability: Flash Deals (Lightning Deals) are limited to a certain time and stock. If a flash deal product added to the cart is not purchased within 15 minutes, it is deleted from the cart and passed on to the next customer.
4. Quantity Limit: In order to prevent unfair purchases (black market) of opportunity products, a purchase limit of 1 or 2 items per customer may be applied.
