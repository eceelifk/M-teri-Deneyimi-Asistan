Q1: When will my order be delivered?
A1: The delivery time of your order depends on the shipping method you choose and your shipping address. You can track your cargo on the My Orders page.

Q2: How much are the shipping charges?
A2: Standard delivery fee applies to orders below a certain amount. Amazon Prime members benefit from free and fast shipping.

Q3: How can I return my order?
A3: You can start the return process by selecting the product you want to return from the My Orders page and clicking the "Return Product" button.

Q4: How many days is the return period?
A4: Most products purchased through Amazon.com.tr can be returned within 30 days from the delivery date.

Q5: Who pays the return shipping fee?
A5: Return shipping costs for defective, damaged or incorrectly shipped products are covered by Amazon. Seller policies may apply for arbitrary returns.

Q6: When will I get my refund?
A6: After the returned product reaches our warehouse and is approved, your refund will be transferred to your bank within 3-5 business days.

Q7: What payment methods do you accept?
A7: You can pay with credit card, debit card and Amazon gift cards. We do not have the option to pay at the door.

Q8: How can I cancel my order?
A8: You can cancel your orders that have not reached the shipping preparation stage from the "My Orders" page. If it has been shipped, the return process must be followed.

Q9: How can I access my invoice?
A9: You can download your electronic invoice by going to the My Orders section and clicking on the "Invoice" option next to the relevant order.

Q10: What is Amazon Prime?
A10: Amazon Prime is a subscription service that offers free fast shipping, Prime Video, Prime Gaming and special discounts for members.

Q11: How can I cancel my Prime membership?
A11: You can cancel or renew your membership at any time from the My Account > Prime Membership tab.

Q12: My product arrived damaged, what should I do?
A12: You can request an exchange or refund for damaged products from the My Orders page within 30 days.

Q13: How can I contact the seller?
A13: To contact marketplace sellers, you can click on the seller's name on the My Orders page and use the "Ask a Question" button.

Q14: How does the warranty process work?
A14: Electronic products are under standard manufacturer's warranty. Your warranty certificate is valid together with your invoice. You can contact authorized services.

Q15: Where can I access the KVKK clarification text?
A15: You can access details about the processing of your personal data from the "Privacy Declaration" and "KVKK" links at the bottom of our site.

Q16: Is my credit card information safe?
A16: Yes, Amazon protects your card information using SSL encryption technologies that comply with global security standards.

Q17: How can I update my address?
A17: You can add a new address or delete your existing addresses from the My Account > My Addresses section.

Q18: Can you gift wrap?
A18: You can have your products gift wrapped and a gift note added by selecting the "This is a gift" option at the cart stage.

Q19: I forgot my password, what should I do?
A19: You can send a password reset link to your e-mail or phone by clicking the "I forgot my password" link on the login screen.

Q20: Why was my account locked?
A20: Accounts may be temporarily locked if a security breach or suspicious activity is detected. You should contact customer service.

Q21: How are customs duties paid for products coming from abroad (Amazon Global)?
A21: Estimated customs duty (Import Fee Deposit) is collected in advance at the basket stage. You don't pay any extra fee.

Q22: What happens if the customs duty is less than estimated?
A22: If the import fee deposit exceeds the actual customs duty, the difference will be automatically refunded to your card.

Q23: How can I reach customer service?
A23: You can request a phone call, connect to live chat, or send an email via the Help > Contact Us page.
Q24: Is there same day delivery?
A24: Same-day or next-day delivery is available for Amazon Prime members in certain regions and on select products.

Q25: The price of the product has decreased, can I get a refund for the difference?
A25: Amazon does not have a refund policy against price drops. If you wish, you can return it and buy it again at the new price.

Q26: Can I change the shipping address after placing an order?
A26: You can change your address before the order proceeds to the cargo preparation phase. No changes can be made afterwards.

Q27: What is the shipping time for marketplace sellers?
A27: Marketplace sellers' shipping times depend on their own policies and are stated as estimated delivery on the product detail page.

Q28: How to print a return label?
A28: When your return request is approved, you will be provided with a return label and shipping code. You can print this and paste it on the package.

Q29: I don't have a printer to print Barcodes/Labels, what should I do?
A29: For some cargo companies, you do not need to print a label, just tell the return code given to the cargo officer.

Q30: Is the product I received original?
A30: All products sold on Amazon.com.tr are original. Selling counterfeit products is against our platform rules and is immediately detected and blocked.

Q31: When can I receive products that are out of stock?
A31: For products that are out of stock, you can use the "Notify me when in stock" button.

Q32: How long can I keep the items in my cart?
A32: The products in your cart will not be deleted, but their prices and availability may change over time.

Q33: How can I use the coupon code?
A33: You can apply your discount by entering your code in the "Add Gift Card or Promotion Code" field at checkout.

Q34: Can I use multiple gift cards at the same time?
A34: Yes, you can combine and use the entire gift card balance defined in your account in a single order.

Q35: What happens if I am not at home during delivery?
A35: The courier may leave your package to a safe place or to your neighbor, or may try to deliver again the next day.

Q36: What is Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)?
A36: It is a system where sellers keep their products in Amazon warehouses and Amazon undertakes shipping/customer service operations.

Q37: Is my personal data shared with third parties?
A37: Amazon shares your personal data in accordance with KVKK rules only for the purpose of improving service quality and delivering your orders.

Q38: How can I change my cookie policy settings?
A38: You can manage your cookie preferences in the Privacy settings in the My Account section.

Q39: What should I do if I threw away the shipping box during the return process?
A39: Products without the original box can be returned by packaging them securely in a different box.

Q40: Can used product be returned?
A40: Except for electronics and personal care products, products in resellable condition can be returned. See the return policy for details.

Q41: My money was refunded but I cannot see it in my account?
A41: Depending on your bank's processes, it may take 1-2 billing cycles for the refund to be reflected on your credit card statement.

Q42: Can I shop without membership?
A42: No, you need to create a free Amazon account to place orders and track cargo.

Q44: I am a taxpayer, can a corporate invoice be issued?

Q47: What are the live support working hours?

Q48: How can I save the products I like?

A52: No, Amazon Prime membership; Prime Express Shipping offers Prime Video and Prime Gaming services all for a single monthly or annual fee.
