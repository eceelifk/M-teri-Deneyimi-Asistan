AMAZON PRIME BENEFITS AND TERMS

Amazon Prime is a subscription program that offers its members many privileges in shopping and entertainment.

1. Free and Fast Shipping Advantage
- Same Day Delivery: In certain cities, same day delivery is free for orders placed before valid hours.
- Next Day Delivery: Free next day delivery opportunity for thousands of products.
- Standard Delivery: Always free shipping on items shipped by Amazon, regardless of cart amount.

2. Prime Video
Prime members can watch Amazon Originals, award-winning movies and popular TV shows on Prime Video at no extra cost.

3. Prime Gaming
Free PC games every month, a free subscription to a streamer every month on Twitch.tv, and in-game loot on popular games.

4. Special Discounts (Prime Day)
Only Prime members are given the right to benefit from early access and great discounts in "Prime Day" events and "Deals of the Day".

5. Membership Cancellation
Prime membership is commitment-free. You can cancel at any time from the "My Account" > "Prime Membership" menu. In case of cancellation, the fee for unused days within that month may be refunded.
