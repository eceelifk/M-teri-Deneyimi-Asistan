AMAZON TURKEY INSTALLMENT AND PAYMENT OPTIONS GUIDE

Payment Methods:
Credit cards and debit cards with Visa, MasterCard, American Express logos are accepted at Amazon.com.tr. 
There are no payment options such as cash on delivery, money order/EFT, BKM Express or cryptocurrency.

Installation Opportunities:
Installment opportunities are offered for credit cards within the framework of the legal regulations of the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (CBRT) and BRSA:
- Interest-Free Installments: Interest-free installments (at cash price) can be made to certain banks' credit cards for up to 3 or 6 months.
- Legal Limitations: Maximum 6 installments for products such as electronic goods, computers, tablets; A maximum of 9 installments can be applied for white goods and furniture.
- Mobile Phone Installments: Maximum 12 installments for mobile phones whose price is below the legally determined limit (eg: 12,000 TL); For those above, a maximum of 3 installments can be made.
- Products that cannot be paid in installments: Installments cannot be made for food, cosmetics, office supplies, gift cards, and products shipped from abroad (Amazon Global). Installations cannot be made using bank cards.

Security and 3D Secure:
All credit card payments are encrypted with 256-bit SSL certificates. When Amazon detects a suspicious transaction, it stops the transaction and sends a 3D Secure (SMS confirmation) request to your bank for your approval. The 3D Secure screen may not appear for every purchase, this completely depends on your bank's risk analysis.

Invoice Transactions:
Corporate Customers (Taxpayers) can request an e-Invoice/corporate invoice by entering their corporate addresses and tax numbers during the order. Invoices are uploaded to the system within 3 days at the latest after the product is delivered and can be downloaded as PDF under "My Orders".
