AMAZON.COM.TR PRIVACY NOTIFICATION AND KVKK (PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION LAW) POLICY

Data Controller:
Your personal data is processed by Amazon Turkey Retail Services Limited Company (“Amazon Turkey”) as the data controller in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”).

What Personal Data Do We Collect?
We collect personal data from you as we provide and continually improve the Amazon Services:
- Information You Provide to Us: Orders, shipping addresses, payment information (credit card), account profile information and conversations with customer service.
- Automatically Collected Information: IP address, browser type, shopping history and product search data collected through cookies.
- Information from Other Sources: Updated delivery addresses from cargo companies.

For what purposes do we process your personal data?
Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:
1. Purchase and delivery of products and services.
2. Payment processing and fraud prevention.
3. Providing customer communications (e-mail, phone, live chat).
4. Providing you with special product and service recommendations (personalization).
5. Fulfillment of legal obligations (invoking, consumer rights legislation).

Do We Share Personal Data?
Amazon does not sell your personal data. Your data is only shared in the following cases:
- Cargo and Delivery Companies: Your address and contact information are shared with cargo companies (e.g. Kolay Gelsin, MNG Cargo, DHL) to deliver your orders.
- Third Party Sellers: When you buy a product from a seller via Marketplace, your order information is shared with the seller to complete the transaction.
- Legal Obligations: Information is shared in line with court decisions or legal requests of authorized public institutions.

Data Security:
Amazon uses advanced SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption and other industry standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss or alteration. Your credit card numbers are fully encrypted and are not kept publicly available in our databases.

What are your rights? (KVKK Article 11)
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- Learning whether your data is being processed or not,
- Requesting information about this if it has been processed,
- Learning the purpose of processing and whether it is used in accordance with its purpose,
- Request correction if it has been processed incompletely or incorrectly,
- Requesting its deletion or destruction within the framework of KVKK conditions.

To exercise these rights, you can visit the "Privacy Settings" section of your account or contact Amazon Customer Service directly.
