AMAZON.COM.TR RETURN POLICY

Return Period:
Most products sold and shipped by Amazon.com.tr can be returned within 30 days from the date the product was delivered to you. 
The products to be returned must be new, unused, complete (with all accessories, tags and packaging) as received.

How Does the Return Process Work?
1. Go to the My Orders page.
2. Find the order you want to return and click the "Return Item" or "Exchange" button.
3. Select the return reason (for example: Product is defective, I changed my mind, wrong item arrived).
4. Once the return is approved, you will receive a return shipping label. Deliver the product to the cargo company with this label.

Who Pays the Shipping Fee?
- Return shipping costs for defective, damaged, incomplete or incorrectly shipped products are covered by Amazon.
- Standard returns are generally free for products sold by Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) or Amazon (when using a contracted courier company).
- In cases of arbitrary returns from third-party marketplace sellers, shipping costs may vary depending on seller policies.

Non-Returnable Products:
Returns for the following product groups are generally not accepted (unless the product is defective):
- Audio and video recordings, software programs (CD, DVD, computer games) whose packaging has been opened within the right of withdrawal period.
- Unpackaged hygiene and personal care products (toothbrush, shaver, earbuds, etc.).
- Food, beverages or supplements that are perishable or likely to expire.
- Digital gift cards and downloadable software.
- Products specially produced or personalized according to your wishes.

Refunds:
The refund is approved after the returned product reaches Amazon or the seller's warehouse and the condition is checked.
- Credit Card/Debit Card: After your return is approved, the amount will be transferred to your bank within 3 to 5 business days.
- Gift Card Balance: For payments made by gift card, the refund amount will be restored to your Amazon account as a gift balance immediately after approval (within 2-3 hours).

Marketplace (Third Party) Seller Refunds:
Returns of products purchased from marketplace sellers are subject to the seller's own return policies, but these policies cannot be stricter than Amazon's standard return policy. If the seller does not respond to a return request or you experience problems, you can request support from Amazon under the "A to Z Guarantee".
