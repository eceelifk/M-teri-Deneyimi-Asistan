Amazon.com.tr Ordering Guide

You can follow the basic steps below to order via Amazon.com.tr:
1. Log In or Create an Account: Go to Amazon.com.tr. If you do not have an account, create a new account from the "Your Account" menu; If so, log in.
2. Search for a Product: Type the name of the product you want to buy in the search bar.
3. Add to Cart: After selecting the product you want, click the "Add to Cart" button on the product page.
4. Complete the Order: To complete your purchase, go to your cart and click the "Complete Order" (or "Proceed to checkout") button.
5. Address and Payment Information: Select your delivery address or enter a new address, then select your payment method (credit card, etc.).
6. Confirm: After checking your order summary, complete the process by clicking the "Confirm order" button.

Help and Support:
If you have any problems with the ordering process or your orders, you can access Amazon's help pages via:
- Online Help Center: You can visit the "Help" or "Customer Services" sections on Amazon.com.tr, select the title appropriate to the problem you are experiencing and get guidance, or contact customer representatives via "Contact Us".
- Contact Form: You can create a support request regarding your orders via amazon.com.tr/iletisim page.
- In-App Support: You can directly get live support or report a problem by using the "Customer Services" option in the side menu of the Amazon mobile application.

*Note: If you are a seller, you must log in to the "Order Management" page via the "Seller Central" panel for operations such as order management and cargo confirmation.
