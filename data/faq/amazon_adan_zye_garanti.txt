AMAZON A TO Z WARRANTY TERMS

What is A to Z Warranty?
Timely delivery and condition of the products you purchase from Amazon.com.tr marketplace sellers (third party sellers) are covered by Amazon's "A to Z Guarantee". When you have a problem and your problem is not solved by the seller, Amazon steps in and protects you.

In Which Situations Is It Valid?
1. Product Not Delivered: If the product has not arrived even though the estimated delivery date has passed in the cargo tracking.
2. Defective/Wrong Product: If the product received is different from the descriptions on the site, damaged, missing or defective.
3. Return Problems: If you sent the product back to the seller within the time specified in the return policy, but the seller did not refund your money or refused.
4. Extra Fees: If you are asked for extra customs or shipping fees outside the site during delivery.

How to Apply?
1. First, you must contact the seller from the My Orders page to solve the problem.
2. If 48 hours have passed since you contacted the seller and a satisfactory solution has not been provided, you can click the "Create a Warranty Claim from A to Z" button.
3. Your request is reviewed by Amazon experts. If you are found right, your money will be refunded by Amazon without waiting for the seller's approval.

Situations Outside the Scope of Warranty:
Digital products, gift cards, or products that are physically damaged due to user error may not be covered by the general warranty. If more than 90 days have passed since the order, a request cannot be created.
