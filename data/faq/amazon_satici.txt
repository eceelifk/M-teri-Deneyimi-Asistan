Becoming a Seller and Selling on Amazon Turkey

Below are the basic steps and resources you can follow to become a seller and start selling on Amazon Turkey:

1. Registration and Getting Started
- Creating a Seller Account: To start selling on Amazon Turkey, you must first create a seller account via the Amazon Seller Central Turkey page.
- Help and Guides: You can review the "Help" sections or the "Start Guide" in Seller Central to get detailed information on topics such as the application process, account management and sales preparation.

2. Sales Models and Logistics
- Amazon Logistics (FBA): It is a service that allows Amazon to manage the storage, packaging, shipping and customer service processes of your products. It offers fast delivery advantage to Prime members.
- Ship It Yourself: You can also choose to manage orders and shipping processes with your own contracted cargo companies.
- Amazon Easy Shipment: It is a delivery assistance service offered for domestic sellers, where contracted carrier companies pick up the packages from your warehouse and deliver them to the buyer.

3. Sales Process and Fees
- Sales Commissions: Amazon receives a sales commission for each product sold, at varying rates depending on the category (usually between 6% and 15%).
- Pricing: Monthly subscription fees or per-sale deductions may apply depending on Seller account type (e.g. Professional Seller account).
- Product Listing: You can list your products, manage your inventory and track your sales reports via Seller Central.

4. Support and Communication
- Contact Us: You can use Amazon's Contact Us forms or support pages for your pre-registration questions or information about account processes.
- Seller Forums: You can visit Amazon Seller Forums to interact with other sellers and benefit from experiences.
